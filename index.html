About

I am a Data Scientist focused on applied machine learning and agentic AI, where I build end-to-end modeling workflows and LLM-powered prototypes that turn messy data into usable decisions. With experience across regression, clustering, time-series forecasting, and neural networks, I emphasize sound evaluation and clear communication in all my work.

Recently, I have been exploring agent workflows using tools such as LangGraph and LangChain (tool use, routing, structured outputs) and translating experiments into practical internal demos. I'm interested in roles that sit at the intersection of ML engineering and analytics, especially where rapid experimentation meets production constraints.

My work spans multiple domains including production MLOps, RAG systems, econometric analysis, and real-time streaming applications. I focus on building systems that bridge the gap between research and practical business applications.
